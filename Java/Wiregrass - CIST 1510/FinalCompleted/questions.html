Common Asked Questions

Are Morty and Crush twins? Answer

Yes, Crush and Morty are twins. They were actually in a litter of 5 kittens that we addopted from a police station. They would find and take in wild cats.

What type of dog is Jack? Answer

Jack is a mixture of Greyhound and lab. We are not 100% sure becasue we got him from a guy giving away puppies outside of Walmart. We just title him a Heinz 57.

Where do you get your pets? Answer

All our pets (except Rocky and Squiggles) are rescues. We always rescue and try to pick the pets that might not be picked. Roscoe was at the Humane Society for a long time, and no one was planning on pickig him up. For that reason we chose him since his time there was almost up.

What do you feed Rocky? Answer

Besides eating as much grass as he wants from the backyard, we feed him lots of vegetables and flowers. The vegetables consists of broccoli, cucumbers, kale, carrots, squash, zucchini, and spinach. The flowers he likes are hibiscus, roses, and sweet potato leaves. Every once in awhile we give him treats that consist of watermellon rinds and pumpkin.

How do all the pets react with Rocky? Answer

All the pets get along great with Rocky. They all grew up with him so they are used to him. At times though Jack will forget about him and assume it's a moving rock. He freaks out a bit but then seems to remember.
