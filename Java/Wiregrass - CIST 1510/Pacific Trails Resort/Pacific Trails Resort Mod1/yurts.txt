	<h2>The Yurts at Pacific Trails Resort</h2>
		<dd><strong>What is a yurt?</strong></dd>
			<dt>Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.</dt>
		<dd><strong>How are yurts furnished?</strong></dd>
			<dt>Each yurt is furnished with a queen-sized bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping experience also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restrooms facilities are located in the lodge.</dt>
		<dd><strong>What should I bring?</strong></dd>
			<dt>Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.</dt>
