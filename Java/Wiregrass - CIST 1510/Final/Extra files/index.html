That Start of Quigley's Crazy Zoo

The Quigley Zoo is a medley of pets. We started out with rescuing Snowball from Alaska. At that time we had two other cats and one dog. Over time the two older cats and dog passed away, leaving Snowball alone. When at a pet store, we saw a Sulcata Tortoise that was the size of a silver dollar. What else could we do but take him home? Later, after rescuing a mother cat and her kittens, we were once again filling our home. We found good homes for all of the kittens and the mom except Crush and Morty. Around this time we fell in love with a puppy being given away at Walmart. Here enters Jack. Soon we realized that Jack needed a friend that wasn't a cat, so we rescued Roscoe.
